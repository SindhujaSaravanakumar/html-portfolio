The Best Movies According to Sindhuja

My top 3 movies of all time

Madrasapattinam

Beautiful love story between an Indian man and a British woman.
The story takes place in the period of 1947.

Sanam Teri Kasam

A musical tale of love , longing and loss.

Mehandi Circus

A music shop owner falls in love with a girl from a circus troupe that has come to his village. But caste and class stand in the way.
Movie shows the reunion of lovers after a traggic phase in each other's life
